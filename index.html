Before we continue

A note on your data

Your answers to the palate questions help your guide prepare a tasting and narrative path tailored to your taste. Before you begin, we'd like to be clear about what we collect, why, and how it is used.

  • What we collect: your contact information, palate preferences, and visit details.
  • Why: to confirm your booking, brief your guide, and (with your permission) keep you informed about ARARAT.
  • How long we keep it: for as long as you remain a registered guest. You can request deletion at any time.
  • Who we share it with: only the ARARAT Museum guide team and our authorized booking and CRM partners.
  • Your rights: access, correction, deletion, and portability under Armenian Law HO-49-N and (for EU/EEA visitors) the GDPR.
